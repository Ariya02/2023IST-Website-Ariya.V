Fennec Fox

Fennec Foxes, also known as Vulpes zerda, are animals native to North African deserts. Their habitats are located throughout the Sahara Desert. The prefer sandy desert resgions with desert grasses or scrub vegetation.

Characteristics

Fennec Foxes weigh around 0.68kg to 1.6kg with a height of 20cm. They are omnivores, eating insects such as grasshoppers and locusts as well as small rodents, lizards and birds
